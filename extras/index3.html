COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.

This disease causes respiratory illness (pke the flu) with symptoms such as cough , fever and in more cases , difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing hands frequently , avoiding touching the face and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

How it spreads?

Corona disease spreads primilary through contact with an infeted person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a subject or object that has the virus on it , then touches the eyes , nose or mouth.
